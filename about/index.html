About Binoio

The Binoio Family

The Binoio Family of Apps is a collection of tools designed with a focus on simplicity, utility, and elegance. We believe that technology should empower users without being overwhelming.

Each member of the family—from Edith’s distraction-free writing to Donots’ sweet task management—is crafted to be “professionally playful.”

Our Philosophy

  • Simplicity: We strip away the noise and focus on what matters.
  • Elegance: A clean design is a functional design.
  • Utility: We solve real-world problems.

The Developer

Hi! I’m the developer behind Bino.io. I’m passionate about building software that people actually enjoy using. I love exploring new technologies and finding better ways to solve everyday challenges.

I’m a big believer in the power of open-source software and self-hosting. That’s why Bino.io is a public repository that I pull onto my own server.

Feel free to explore my projects on GitHub.
